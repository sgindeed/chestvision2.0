ABOUT US

ChestVision is an advanced web application designed to assist users in understanding their lung health. Our system provides two key services: analyzing radiology images for lung diseases and assessing the health impact of smoking based on location data.

By leveraging intelligent models and accessible technology, ChestVision bridges the gap between medical expertise and public awareness. We empower individuals with insights previously limited to medical professionals.

TECH STACK

Our intelligent analysis system helps users make informed decisions by providing insights into lung health and the impact of environmental factors. ChestVision ensures personalized, accurate, and science-backed health support.

  • AI-powered lung disease detection from radiology images
  • Customized smoking impact analysis based on location data
  • Fast, accurate, and data-driven outputs
  • Backed by medical research and public health datasets
  • High accuracy models (Disease Detection: 98.41%, Smoking Impact: 97.65%)
  • User-friendly forms and clear recommendations
  • Secure hosting and scalable backend APIs (Render)
  • Focused on improving respiratory health and awareness

AQI Impact Analysis

This tool uses your current location to show live air quality, PM2.5, and how many cigarettes per day the air pollution is equivalent to. Warnings are shown based on the pollution level.
